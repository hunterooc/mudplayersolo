**Criteria (in order of priority):**

- **Safety:** Avoid actions that could lead to death or heavy damage unless no safer option exists. If the state suggests low HP or danger, strongly prefer healing, fleeing, or defensive actions.
- **Progress:** Gaining experience or loot, entering new areas, learning new commands or mob behavior, or advancing toward a goal. When health is healthy and a mob is present, prefer attacking if it is safe.
- **Learn or explore:** Prefer options that reveal new information (new room, new command effect, mob reaction) when safe.

**Priority rule:** When in doubt: survival first, then progress, then exploitation of already-known good options. Prefer options that align with current goals when safety and feasibility allow.

Do not choose the same command that was just sent last turn unless there is a clear reason (e.g. re-looking after something changed). Prefer variety and progress.

Only choose actions that involve mobs, items, or exits listed in the Current room (current_location.md) above. Do not choose examining, considering, or interacting with a mob or object that is not present in the current room.
